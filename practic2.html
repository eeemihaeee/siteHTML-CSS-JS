TAG

noun
  1. A label attached to someone or something for the purpose of identification or to give other information.
    "He gave his pet a callar with a metal name tag"
    synonyms: label, ticket, badge, mark, marker, tab, tally, docket, stub, chit, chitt, counterfoi, flag, stamp, sticker
    "A price tag"
  2. A small piece or part thet is attached to a main body.
verb
  1. Attach a label to.
    "Mothers suspected thet their babies had been wringly tagged during an alarm at the hospital"
    synonyms: label, ticket, attach tags to, put a label on, mark, earmark, identify, docket, flag, indicate
    "The bottles were tagged with colour-coded labels"
  2. Add to something, especially as an afterthought or with no real connection
    "She meant to tag her question on at the end of her remarks"
    synonyms: add, tack, join, attach, append, affix annex
    "Their heads were so large thet the rest of their bodies seemed tagged on as an afterhought"
